Our Services

Comprehensive beauty services from our talented team of professionals

Christopher J Salon offers a complete range of professional beauty services. Our experienced team stays current with the latest trends and techniques to ensure you receive exceptional results every time.

Hair Color Services

Expert color services including balayage, foiling, color correction, and highlights. From subtle enhancements to bold transformations, our color specialists use advanced techniques and chemistry knowledge to create stunning, long-lasting results tailored to your unique style.

Haircuts & Styling

Precision cutting for all hair types and styles. Our experienced stylists specialize in consultations to create looks that fit your lifestyle and are easy to maintain at home. From classic cuts to modern styles, we deliver exceptional results.

Hair Extensions

Premium quality hair extensions for added length, volume, and dimension. Our extension experts ensure seamless blending with your natural hair for beautiful, natural-looking results that enhance your unique style.

Men's Grooming

Specialized men's cuts and grooming services from our men's specialist. Expert precision cutting, fades, and styling tailored specifically for men, ensuring you look sharp and polished.

Nail Services

Complete nail care including manicures, pedicures, and nail art. Our nail specialists provide exceptional service with attention to detail, focusing on both beauty and health. We emphasize the importance of proper foot care.

Hair Texturizing

Expert texturizing services to add movement, reduce bulk, or create specific textures. Our stylists use advanced techniques to enhance your hair's natural beauty and make styling easier.

Deva Curl Specialist

Specialized curly hair cutting and styling using the Deva Curl method. Our certified stylists understand the unique needs of curly hair and create cuts that enhance your natural curl pattern.

Up-styling & Special Events

Professional up-dos and styling for weddings, proms, and special occasions. Our stylists create beautiful, long-lasting styles that photograph beautifully and stay perfect throughout your event.

Waxing Services

Professional waxing services for eyebrows and other areas. Quick, efficient service from experienced professionals who prioritize your comfort.

Trichology & Scalp Care

Specialized scalp and hair health services from our triple-certified trichologist. Expert care for fine hair, sensitive scalps, and various hair and scalp concerns with a gentle touch and detailed approach.

Makeup Services

Professional makeup application including airbrush services. Perfect for special events, photoshoots, or learning new techniques for everyday beauty.

Personalized Consultations

Detailed consultations to understand your hair goals, lifestyle, and maintenance preferences. We collaborate with you to create customized plans that deliver results you'll love and can maintain at home.
